DO'S

Octopi ;) are residents of the ocean, an ecosystem which is in serious danger! Educate yourself about this habitat. If you have the possibility you might try snorkeling or scuba diving (best in a non commercial dive center and without touching or removing anything) to understand and appreciate this ecosystem. There are online courses for awareness and education from diving associations like SSI.

How can YOU help octopi?

  • limit your seafood consume
  • avoid single used plastic
  • avoid fast fashion (micro plastic)...and avoid products with micro plastic in general
  • don’t touch or remove any corals etc.
  • lower your CO2 footprint -> it heats up the oceans
