Want to go out?

Berlin gets you a little stir-crazy and you just want to get out and relax? How about a hike in the Elbe Sandstone Mountains? Have already been there? Maybe you'll find new inspiration at the University Hiking Club. That way you'll find a group that shares the love to explore right away and it won't fail at the organization stage ;)

In Berlin fällt dir die Decke auf den Kopf und du möchtest einfach mal raus und abschalten? Wie wärs mit einer Wanderung im Elbsandsteingebirge? Da warst du schon? Vielleicht findest du neue Inspirationen beim Uni-Wanderclub. So findest du auch direkt eine Truppe und es scheitert nicht schon an der Organisation ;)

DO'S

  • If you choose to join a group, respect the others and be open to learn from each other.
  • A lot of destinations are reachable by train which is more sustainable than to go there by car.

DONT'S

  • We enjoy being in nature because it’s so pure and untouched - keep it like that!
